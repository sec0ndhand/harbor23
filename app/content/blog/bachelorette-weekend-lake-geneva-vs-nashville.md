## The Nashville default, and why Chicago brides are breaking it

For the last decade, the default Chicago bachelorette itinerary has been the same: book flights to Nashville, pile into a downtown hotel, pub crawl Broadway, wake up in a pedal tavern photo. It's fun. It's also expensive once you add flights, hotel, drinks, dinners, and transportation.

There's a better version of the same weekend 90 minutes from home. Here's why.

## Lake Geneva vs Nashville — the honest breakdown

| | Lake Geneva | Nashville |
|---|---|---|
| **Flights** | None — 90 min drive from Chicago | Round-trip airfare per person |
| **Lodging** | Whole lake house, 4BR, sleeps 14 | Hotel (2 per room) or downtown Airbnb |
| **Bar scene** | Wine bars, cocktail lounges, lakefront patios | Broadway bachelorette strip |
| **Day activities** | Boat day, spa, winery, lake swim | Honky tonks, shopping, more honky tonks |
| **Cost per person** | ~$400–600 for 3 nights | Significantly more with flights and hotel |
| **Vibe** | Intimate, private, whole house together | Loud, crowded, shared spaces |

The pitch is simple: you get the same "whole-weekend-together" feeling, minus the airport chaos and the flight bill.

## What a Lake Geneva bachelorette weekend actually looks like

### Friday — arrive, unpack, toast

The crew rolls in from Chicago in the late afternoon. You walk into a 4-bedroom house you have **to yourselves** — no shared hotel walls, no lobby small talk — and immediately claim beds, pop the first bottle, and head to the patio. Dinner is either at **[Sopra Bistro](https://soprabistro.com/)** (Italian, great pasta) or **[Oakfire](https://www.oakfire.pizza/)** (wood-fired Neapolitan pizza right on Wrigley Drive with lake views). First-night plan: cocktails and wine bars in downtown Lake Geneva — everything is within walking distance.

### Saturday — spa morning, lake afternoon, dinner, dancing

The winning bachelorette Saturday has three chapters:

- **Morning:** Spa day at **[Avani Spa](https://www.theabbeyresort.com/lake-geneva-wisconsin-spa/)** inside The Abbey Resort in Fontana. Book massages as a group and linger at the pools — it's a 35,000-square-foot facility, one of the nicest in the region.
- **Afternoon:** **[Geneva Lake Distilling + Winery](https://www.visitlakegeneva.com/listing/geneva-lake-distilling-+-winery/462/)** for a tasting, then back to the house for pool/lake time. Some groups rent a boat from **[Gordy's Marine](https://www.gordysboats.com/)** in Fontana instead — same relaxed vibe, wetter.
- **Evening:** Private chef dinner at the house, or reservations at **[Pier 290](https://pier290.com/)** on the water in Williams Bay — the only waterfront restaurant on Geneva Lake. End the night downtown or around the fire pit with cocktails.

### Sunday — brunch, photos, home

Brunch somewhere with a view (**[Daddy Maxwell's](https://www.yelp.com/biz/daddy-maxwells-diner-and-cafe-williams-bay)** in Williams Bay is the local favorite for homemade donuts and breakfast), group photos by the lake, then the easiest-ever "trip home" — everyone's in their own bed in Chicago by Sunday evening.
